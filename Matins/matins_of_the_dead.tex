\chapter*{Matins of the Dead}

 \textit{On All Souls' Day, all three Nocturns are said. On oher days, only one Nocturn is said according to the following order: first Nocturn on Sundays, Mondays, and Thursdays; second Nocturn on Tuesdays and Fridays; third Nocturn on Wednesdays and Saturdays. On the day of burial, the first Nocturn is always said.}
